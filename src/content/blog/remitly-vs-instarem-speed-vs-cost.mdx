When sending money from Australia to India, speed and cost are often the two most critical factors. Two platforms that consistently rank highly for these features are **Remitly** and **Instarem**. 

Both offer excellent digital-first experiences, but they cater to slightly different needs. Should you prioritize Instarem's consistent mid-market-like rates or Remitly's promotional offers and express delivery?

In this guide, we'll break down how they compare in 2026.

## 1. Exchange Rates and Hidden Fees

**Instarem** is known for offering zero-margin exchange rates (they give you the real mid-market rate) and charging a transparent, upfront fee. This makes it incredibly easy to see exactly what you are paying for the transfer.

**Remitly**, on the other hand, makes its money through an exchange rate margin. The rate you see on Remitly is slightly lower than the mid-market rate, but they often advertise "Zero Fees" on specific transfer types. 

**The Verdict on Cost:** For consistently large transfers, Instarem's transparent fee model usually wins out, as exchange rate margins (like Remitly's) eat into larger amounts more aggressively. However, Remitly frequently runs promotional rates for first-time users that are unbeatable. Always compare them live on **RemitIQ** before sending.

## 2. Transfer Speed

If you need the money to arrive *right now*, **Remitly** offers an "Express" option that delivers funds to Indian bank accounts or UPI IDs in minutes. This speed is incredibly reliable, though it sometimes comes with a marginally worse exchange rate or a slightly higher fee.

**Instarem** is also very fast, typically delivering funds to India on the same day or the next business day. While rarely instant, it is more than fast enough for 95% of use cases (like sending money for family maintenance or savings).

**The Verdict on Speed:** Remitly takes the crown for emergency transfers. If the recipient needs funds in the next 10 minutes, Remitly's Express service is the way to go.

## 3. Ease of Use and Platform Features

Both platforms have excellent mobile apps with millions of positive reviews. 

- **Remitly** is often praised for its incredibly intuitive interface, making it easy for non-tech-savvy users to navigate.
- **Instarem** offers a slightly more "pro" feel, giving users detailed breakdowns of fees and offering rewards (InstaPoints) that can mitigate the cost of future transfers.

## Final Thoughts

There is no definitive "winner" between Remitly and Instarem; it entirely depends on your specific transfer at that exact moment.

- Choose **Remitly** if you are a first-time user looking for a great promotional rate, or if you need the money to arrive instantly via their Express service.
- Choose **Instarem** if you are sending larger amounts and want the transparency of real mid-market exchange rates with a flat fee.
